Soyeht

iOS & macOS terminal client for theyOS servers.

Soyeht is a terminal client that lets you manage remote AI coding agents (Claude Code, Codex, OpenCode) and containerized instances directly from your iPhone or Mac. Built on top of a fork of SwiftTerm.

Download for macOS

Requires macOS 15 or later. Open the DMG and drag Soyeht to Applications.
